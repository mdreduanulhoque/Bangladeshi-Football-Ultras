Discover Our Spirit

Our Unwavering Passion

Born from a deep love for Bangladesh football, the Bangladeshi Football Ultras emerged as a movement to reignite the nation's sporting fervor. We remember the golden era of the 1990s, when stadiums vibrated with energy and rivalries like Abahani and Mohammedan captured the nation's heart.

Our journey began amidst calls for change, driven by a singular vision: to bring back the electrifying atmosphere that inspires our team and intimidates opponents. We believe in the power of collective rhythm, chants, and unwavering support to transform every match into an unforgettable experience.

Our Distinct Identity

The Black Wall

Our signature black attire and bandanas symbolize our unity and formidable presence. We move as one, a sea of black, echoing our determination to stand strong with our team.

Rhythm of Support

Drums, whistles, and powerful chants are the heartbeat of our support. We create an electrifying sonic landscape that fuels our players and reverberates through the stadium.

Visual Spectacle

Elaborate tifos, vibrant smoke, and defiant banners are our visual language. We paint the stands with messages of pride, hope, and unwavering belief in Bangladeshi football.

"No Ultras, No Football!"

Our Unwavering Dedication

Our commitment extends beyond the ninety minutes of a match. We are advocates for the game, tirelessly working to ensure that Bangladeshi football receives the support and recognition it deserves. We've championed players, fought for fair practices, and consistently raised our voices for the betterment of the sport.

From demanding proper ticket allocations to advocating for talented diaspora players like Hamza, we are on the front lines, pushing for a brighter future for the Bengal Tigers. Our dedication is a promise: we will keep fighting for the soul of football in Bangladesh.
